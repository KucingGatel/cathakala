Mechafix

This website was developed to support and simplify motorcycle service needs for customers by providing a reliable and transparent digital platform. Beyond serving end users or consumers, the information system was designed to accommodate multiple roles, including administrators and mechanics, ensuring that all operational processes within the service center can be managed efficiently and systematically.

The responsive website offers a range of features that enable users to monitor and track the performance and progress of mechanics while their motorcycles are being serviced. Through this system, customers can access service updates, view repair statuses, and gain better visibility into the maintenance process, creating a more informed and engaging service experience.

This innovation was introduced in response to common issues within the motorcycle service industry, where unethical practices or dishonest behavior by mechanics may occur for personal gain. By incorporating a monitoring and supervision feature, the MechaFix website aims to prevent such misconduct, promote accountability, and foster trust between service providers and customers, ultimately supporting fair and transparent business practices.
